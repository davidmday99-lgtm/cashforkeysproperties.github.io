Cash Sale Timeline: What to Expect Day by Day

Published: June 2, 2026 By: Cash For Keys Properties

You've heard that cash sales are fast. But how fast, exactly? And what actually happens during the process? Here's a realistic day-by-day breakdown of what to expect when selling your home for cash.

Day 1: Initial Contact

You reach out through our website, call, or text. We'll ask some basic questions:

  • Property address
  • Basic condition (number of beds/baths, major issues)
  • Your timeline and motivation for selling
  • Whether you're the owner and any mortgage/lien situation

This conversation takes about 10-15 minutes. Based on this information, we can often give you a preliminary offer range within hours.

Days 1-2: Property Assessment

We schedule a quick walkthrough of your property. This isn't like a traditional buyer's viewing where we're deciding if we like the paint colors. We're evaluating:

  • Overall condition and needed repairs
  • Size, layout, and features
  • Location factors
  • Anything that affects value

The visit typically takes 15-30 minutes. We don't need the home to be clean or staged—we're buying it as-is, remember?

Day 2-3: Written Offer

After our assessment, we prepare a formal written offer. This includes:

  • The purchase price
  • Proposed closing date (usually 7-14 days, but flexible based on your needs)
  • Any terms or conditions (usually none)
  • Exactly what you'll net after closing costs

There's no obligation. Take time to review, ask questions, and decide. We don't pressure.

Day 3-4: Offer Acceptance

Once you accept, we open escrow and begin the closing process. We'll need:

  • Signed purchase agreement
  • Your identification
  • Any mortgage or lien information
  • Keys and access for final verification

Days 4-10: Title Work

The title company conducts a title search to ensure:

  • Clear ownership
  • No unexpected liens or judgments
  • Property taxes are current (or we know what to pay off)
  • Legal description matches records

This is the main variable in timing. Clean titles clear in 5-7 days. Complicated titles may take longer.

Days 10-14: Closing

At closing, you'll sign documents transferring ownership. Options include:

  • Meet at the title company office
  • Mobile notary comes to you
  • Remote online notarization (in some states)

The signing takes about 30-60 minutes. You'll receive your payment via:

  • Wire transfer (funds available same day)
  • Certified check (if preferred)

What Can Delay Closing?

Most cash sales close in 7-14 days, but some factors can extend the timeline:

  • Title issues: Liens, judgments, or ownership disputes need resolution
  • Probate: Estate sales require court approval
  • Multiple owners: All parties must sign
  • Existing mortgage payoff: Lender processing takes time

We work to resolve issues quickly and keep you informed throughout.

Compare to Traditional Sales

Traditional sale timeline:

  • Weeks 1-2: Prep home, hire agent, take photos
  • Weeks 2-8: List and show property
  • Week 8-10: Receive and negotiate offers
  • Weeks 10-14: Buyer's inspection, appraisal, financing
  • Weeks 14-16: Final negotiations and closing prep
  • Week 16+: Close (if nothing falls through)

That's 4-5 months minimum. Cash sales accomplish in 2 weeks what traditional sales take 4 months to achieve.
